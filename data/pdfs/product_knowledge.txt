NexusAI Product Information
NexusAI is a B2B SaaS platform designed to automate supply chain logistics. 
Our main features include predictive inventory management, automated vendor communication, and real-time fleet tracking.
Pricing:
- Starter Plan: $99/month. Includes basic inventory tracking and email support. Up to 1,000 SKUs.
- Pro Plan: $299/month. Includes predictive analytics, API access, and priority support. Up to 10,000 SKUs.
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing. Includes real-time fleet tracking, dedicated account manager, and custom integrations. Unlimited SKUs.
Recent Case Study:
We helped "GlobalTech Industries" reduce their supply chain costs by 23% in 6 months using our predictive analytics module. They upgraded from the Pro to the Enterprise plan to get the fleet tracking feature.
Important Notes:
NexusAI does not currently support blockchain or crypto payments. All payments are processed via standard credit card or ACH.
